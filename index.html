We Are 4th Family

Leveraging a love of sports to switch on the overlooked aptitude of at-risk youth, 4th Family designs and delivers empowerment: positive, authentically science-based programs in its home town of Albany, NY, and beyond.

S.T.E.M. Programs

We give at-risk youth a gift that lasts a lifetime: the knowledge that science is within reach. The confidence to research, analyze and present data - in context - supports economic mobility, entrepreneurship, social and racial justice. Science belongs to us all; with an understanding of science, we stand tall.
